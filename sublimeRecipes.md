## Column Editing

* Highlight Word<br>

* Add Word:<br>
ctrl+d<br>

* Go to Previous Word:<br>
ctrl+u<br>

* Skip Current Word:<br>
ctrl+k+d<br>

* Open Command Pallet [WIN]:<br>
ctrl+shift+p<br>

* Open Help Search [MAC]:<br>
cmd?+shift+/<br>
